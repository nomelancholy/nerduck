The reason why Nerduck donates :

Fantastic Reality Project

What’s Fantastic Reality Project?

Fantastic Reality Project is to reduce the gap between reality and virtual world and to maintain a connecting link not to make our reality look miserable because of the richness in the virtual world, Metaverse with infinite possibilities.

Nerduck is the first Fantastic Reality Project.

We don’t think our future is the background of the movie <Ready Player One> about the main character, who has an adventure in a fancy virtual world, but lives a poor life in reality. We wish our reality will be abundant as the Metaverse market grows.

So Nerduck returns 50% of earnings to society. It includes loyalty profits. Therefore, donation continues as transaction becomes active.

We settle and donate every quarter(March, June, September and December).

Where do we donate?

For now, we donate to BINANCE CHARITY that opens sponsorship records transparently, via block chains.

If there is a donation platform that has a decentralized management system and is more transparent and reliable than BINANCE CHARITY in future, we will donate to them.
